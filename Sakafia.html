About SOSA 2026

Under the guidance of our Patron MR. ADAM HARUN, SOSA 2026 is the official Old Students' Association for the 2026 year group of Sakafia Islamic Senior High School. Our vision is to foster unity, support our alma mater, and build a strong network for the progress of Great Sakisco.
